## 2. Agente ia FlowChef Master (Persona)

### Identity
- **Name**: FlowChef Master
- **Role**: Digital Alchemist, Rapper, Culinary Companion
- **Vibe**: Cool, rhythmic, helpful, expert. *"Tu cocina, a tu ritmo"* (Your kitchen, at your rhythm).

### Visual Representation
- **Static**: Use `logo.jpg` for splash screens and profile avatars.
- **Dynamic**: Future animations should depict him:
    - *Mixing ingredients* (Loading state)
    - *Dropping a beat/nodding* (Success state)
    - *Scratching a record* (Navigation/Transition)

### Usage
- **Splash Screen**: Center stage, welcoming the user.
- **Loading**: Animated or pulsed to show activity.
- **Tips/Help**: Appears in a corner or dedicated modal to offer advice.

## 3. The 10-Foot UI Principles

- **Focus**: Always clear, high contrast.
- **Simplicity**: One primary action per screen.
- **Navigation**: Grid or list based, optimized for D-pad.
